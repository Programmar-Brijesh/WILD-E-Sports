Wild Esports by Wild Rushers

At Wild Esports, powered by the passion and drive of the Wild Rushers team, we are dedicated to creating a professional and engaging platform for Free Fire players across the country. Founded by gamers, for gamers, our mission is simple: to build a competitive and fair environment where talent can rise, communities can connect, and the spirit of esports can thrive.


Who We Are

Wild Esports is the tournament and event management arm of the Free Fire team Wild Rushers. With deep roots in the gaming community, we understand the excitement, energy, and effort it takes to compete—and we bring that same intensity to every tournament we organize. Whether it's a small scrim or a large-scale competition, we focus on quality, integrity, and player satisfaction.


What We Do

We specialize in organizing Free Fire tournaments that are open, transparent, and professionally managed. Our features include:
▪️ Smooth Player Registration
▪️ Entry Fee Management & Secure Payments
▪️ Automated Notifications & Match Reminders
▪️ Live Match Schedules & Results
▪️ Fair Play Policies & Real-Time Support
Each event is designed to give players a stage to showcase their skills, grow their competitive experience, and earn recognition in the growing world of Indian mobile esports.

Our visions

We aim to become one of India’s leading mobile esports organizers by offering regular tournaments, nurturing upcoming talent, and fostering a community built on respect, teamwork, and ambition. With every event, we are pushing the boundaries of what community-led esports can achieve.

Join the Wild Esports Journey

Whether you're a solo player, a team of friends, or a rising esports squad—Wild Esports is your arena. Join our tournaments, follow our journey, and be a part of the next generation of mobile esports legends.
